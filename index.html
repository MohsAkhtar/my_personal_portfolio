# About Mohs Akhtar

Mohs Akhtar is a computer science graduate from Edinburgh Napier University. He has a strong background with front-end/back-end development, problem solving, and technical writing.

Ever since learning to code his first application at the age of 15, Mohs developed a passion for software and web development that would carrry him on over the next few years.

Proficient in Java, Python, JavaScript and SQL amongst other tools. Mohs has worked on various projects be it group or solo projects. He is familiar with Agile methodolgies as well as team communication tools such as Slack.
